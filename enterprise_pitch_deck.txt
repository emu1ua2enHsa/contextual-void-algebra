SLIDE 2: THE STATUS QUO PROBLEM (THE CRISIS OF ZERO)
- Core Issue: Modern double-entry bookkeeping relies on balance netting (n - n = 0).
- Impact: When records settle to zero, the operational trajectory and historical 
  metadata are discarded. This structural information loss creates vectors for hidden ledger 
  anomalies, compliance leaks, and expensive reconciliation errors.

- Result: 0.0% account reconciliation variance profiles across distributed banking hubs.
