# Wyoming LLC Electronic Filing Protocol

## 1. Accessing Portal Registry
- Navigate your browser to the official **[Wyoming Secretary of State Business Center](https://wyo.gov)**.
- Select "Start a New Business Entity" to initialize the online wizard tracker.

## 2. Input Parameters
- **Entity Type**: Limited Liability Company (Domestic).
- **Business Name**: `IPC QUANTUM TECHNOLOGIES, LLC`.
- **Registered Agent**: Select an authorized local registered agent service company to preserve your personal pseudonymous privacy on public file structures.

## 3. Fee Capital Allocation
- Submit the standard state filing fee ($100 USD) to push the registry items live.
